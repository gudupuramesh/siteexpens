FAQs

Frequently asked questions

What is InteriorOS?

InteriorOS is a project management platform built specifically for interior design studios. It connects your site teams, supervisors, vendors, and finance on one platform — replacing WhatsApp groups, Excel sheets, and Tally with a single system designed for how interior designers actually work.

How is InteriorOS different from generic project management tools?

Unlike Asana, Monday, or Trello, InteriorOS includes interior-specific features: a laminate library with room-by-room selection, whiteboard for sketches, material requests with photo approvals, vendor directory with running balances, and a full financial ledger with GST-ready invoices. Generic tools will never ship these.

Does InteriorOS work on mobile and desktop?

Yes. InteriorOS is available on iOS (App Store), Android (Play Store), and as a web app accessible from any browser. Site supervisors typically use the mobile app for DPRs, attendance, and photos, while studio owners use the web app for dashboards, financials, and planning.

Can my team members join multiple organizations?

Yes. A supervisor or designer can belong to multiple studios with one account. They simply switch between organizations from the app. Each organization's data is completely separate and secure.

How does the financial tracking work?

Every payment in/out is tracked per project in real time. Supervisors submit expenses with photos; you approve before any money is committed. The studio-wide ledger gives you a single view of all projects' finances. You can export payment summary PDFs for clients with one tap.

Is there a free trial?

Yes. Start free — no credit card required. Set up your studio in under 5 minutes, invite your team, and start managing your first project immediately. Upgrade when you're ready.
